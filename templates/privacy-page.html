Commitment to Privacy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit Mackkas.

The information we collect is used to:

Protection of Information

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Your data is stored on secure servers and payment transactions are encrypted using SSL technology.

Care Instructions

Preserving Your Pieces

At Mackkas, we craft our jewelry and garments to last. Proper care ensures your items remain as beautiful as the day you received them.

Garment Care

  • Cotton: Machine wash cold with like colors. Tumble dry low or hang to dry to prevent shrinking.
  • Linen: Hand wash or machine wash on a delicate cycle. Reshape and lay flat to dry. Iron while slightly damp.
  • Wool/Cashmere: Hand wash cold with gentle detergent. Never wring or twist. Lay flat on a towel to dry.
  • Silk: Dry clean recommended. If hand washing, use cold water and silk-safe detergent.

Jewelry Care

  • Silver & Gold: Clean with a soft lint-free cloth. Avoid contact with harsh chemicals, perfumes, and lotions.
  • Gemstones: Wipe with a damp cloth after wearing. Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
  • Storage: Keep your jewelry in individual pouches or a lined jewelry box to prevent scratching and tangling.

General Tips

Always refer to the specific care label attached to your garment for detailed instructions.

  • Wash less frequently to extend the life of your clothes.
  • Use eco-friendly detergents to protect both your garments and the environment.
  • Repair minor damages early to prevent them from becoming larger issues.

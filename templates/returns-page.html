Returns & Exchanges

Our Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not, we accept returns within 14 days of the delivery date, provided the items are in their original condition with all tags attached.

Return Process

To initiate a return, please follow these steps:

  • Visit our Contact page or email concierge@mackkas.com with your order number.
  • Our team will provide you with a return authorization and shipping instructions.
  • Securely pack your items and include the original invoice.

Exchanges

If you would like to exchange an item for a different size or color, please contact our concierge team. We will facilitate the exchange and ensure your new piece arrives promptly.

Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. Approved refunds will be processed to your original method of payment within 5-10 business days.
